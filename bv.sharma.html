Prof. B. V. Sharma

Prof. BV Sharma is one of the distinguished scholars in anthropology in India. He is especially recognized for his contributions in the fields of medical and applied anthropology in India. He is currently the Director of the Anthropological Survey of India (AnSI). Before joining AnSI as its Director, Prof. Sharma served as Professor at the Department of Anthropology, University of Hyderabad, for about 30 years.

He was given a full additional charge as Director General of the Raja Ram Mohan Roy Library Foundation (RRLF) under the Ministry of Culture, Government of India, during 2023-2024. Prof. Sharma recently served as Chairperson of the Sikkim State High Level Committee that was constituted for the preparation of comprehensive reports for the inclusion of 12 ethnic communities of Sikkim in the list of Scheduled Tribes. He served as a member of the Advisor committee for the Tribal Museum in the Rashtrapati Bhavan and a member of the advisor committee for the 6500 crore Marine Heritage Museum being built at Lothal, Gujarat.
