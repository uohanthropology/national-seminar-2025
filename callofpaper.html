Participation Guidelines

🎓 For Academic Participants:

  • ✍️ Research Papers: (3000 - 5000 words)
  • 🖼️ Poster Presentations
  • 🧑‍🎓 Thesis presentations

🤝 For Non-Governmental Organizations:

  • 🗺️ Case Study Presentations
  • 💡 Best Practice Sharing Sessions
  • 🗣️ Community Experience Narratives

⚖️ For Policymakers:

  • 🏛️ Policy Brief Presentations
  • 🤝 Policy-Practice Interface Sessions

🏆 Awards (Students):

  • 🥇 Best Poster
  • 🏅 Best Paper

📝 SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

📄 Abstract Submission Requirements:

  • 📏 Length: 250-300 words
  • ✍️ Format: Single-spaced, 12-point font, 1-inch margins
  • 💡 Content: Research question, methodology, key findings, significance
  • 🔑 Keywords: 4-6 relevant keywords
  • 👤 Author Information: Name, affiliation, contact details, brief bio.

📖 Full Paper Requirements (if accepted):

  • 🔍 Research Papers: 3000-5000 words excluding references
  • 📚 Format: APA style citations and references
