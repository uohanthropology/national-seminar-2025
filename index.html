NATIONAL SEMINAR

on

Anthropology in Contemporary Practice: Pathways for a Sustainable Future

In honour of Prof. B.V. Sharma

26-27 September 2025

Register Now

National Conference

Department of Anthropology, University of Hyderabad cordially invites researchers, academicians, practitioners, students, policymakers, and community representatives to participate in this significant academic event that explores the evolving role of anthropology in addressing contemporary global challenges.

Thematic Areas

  • Medicine and Health
  • Education: Learning, Culture, and Social Transformation
  • Applied Anthropology and Public Engagement
  • Development, SDGs, Policy and Practice
  • Digital Space and Virtual Ethnography
  • Urban dynamics
  • Environment and Climate Change
  • Gender in contemporary practice
  • Museums, Heritage and Arts
  • Any other area related to the theme of the seminar is also welcome
