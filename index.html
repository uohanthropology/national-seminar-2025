Anthropology in Contemporary Practice: Pathways for a Sustainable Future (September 26-27, 2025)

Objectives

Join us for the Two-Day National Seminar on Anthropology in Contemporary Practice: Pathways for a Sustainable Future, hosted by the Department of Anthropology, University of Hyderabad, on September 26-27, 2025. This interdisciplinary forum brings together scholars, practitioners, policymakers, and communities to explore how anthropological insights address pressing challenges in health, education, environment, technology, urban development, and more. Delve into sub-themes like Medicine & Health, Digital Ethnography, and Climate Change through research papers, case studies, and collaborative sessions. Submit abstracts by August 25, 2025, and contribute to actionable pathways for a resilient society!

Sub-Themes & Indicative Focus

Participants are invited to contribute within these areas:

  • Medicine & Health
  • Education & Social Transformation
  • Applied Anthropology
  • Development, SDGs, & Policy
  • Digital Space & Ethnography
  • Urban Dynamics
  • Environment & Climate Change
  • Gender in Practice
  • Museums, Heritage & Arts
  • Open Theme
