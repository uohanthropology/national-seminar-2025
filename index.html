Anthropology in Contemporary Practice: Pathways for a Sustainable Future

About the Seminar

Anthropology today engages with health inequities, educational transformation, environmental risk, digital life, urban change, and heritage futures. This seminar brings together scholars, practitioners, policymakers, and communities to foster problem-focused knowledge and strengthen university-society linkages.
