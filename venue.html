India

India, a land of diverse cultures and heritage, is known for its academic institutions, innovation, and global research contributions.

Hyderabad

Hyderabad, known as the “City of Pearls”, blends tradition and technology. It is home to historic landmarks such as the Charminar and modern IT hubs.

University of Hyderabad

The University of Hyderabad is a premier institution for postgraduate education and research, known for its sprawling green campus and academic excellence.

Who Should Attend?

  • Researchers and Academics
  • Policymakers and Government Officials
  • Undergraduate, Postgraduate and Research Students
  • Practitioners from NGOs and CSOs
  • Corporate and Industry Professionals

Participation Formats

  • Academic: Research papers and posters.
  • NGOs/CSOs: Case studies and field reports.
  • Policymakers: Policy briefs and discussion notes.
  • Students: Special presentation awards for outstanding student papers.
